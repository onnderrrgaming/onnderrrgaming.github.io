Privacy Policy for Diamond Blocks DI Game

Effective Date: 01.10.2024

Introduction

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use our app, Diamond Blocks DI Game, available on the Apple App Store.

Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information directly. However, our app uses Google AdMob and Unity Ads to serve advertisements, which may collect certain information to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

Specifically:

  • Personal Information:
    • We do not request or collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.
  • App Usage Data:
    • Google AdMob and Unity Ads may collect data such as:
    • Device information (e.g., device type, operating system, unique identifiers).
    • IP address.
    • Ad interactions and engagement.

Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with Google AdMob and Unity Ads for monetization. Both AdMob and Unity Ads may collect and process data as part of their ad-serving process. This data collection is governed by their respective privacy policies, not ours.

  • For more details on how Google handles your data, please refer to the Google Privacy Policy.
  • You can also review AdMob's specific data practices in their AdMob Help Center.
  • For information about Unity Ads' data practices, please refer to the Unity Privacy Policy.

Unity Ads Privacy Statement:

Unity Technologies provides the Unity Ads platform to help monetize apps through advertisements. Unity Ads may collect information related to the user's device, including but not limited to:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID, IP address, device model, and operating system version).
  • Interaction with ads (e.g., ad views, clicks, and impressions).
  • Location data (general location data, such as city or country).

Unity Ads may use this data to serve personalized or non-personalized advertisements, analyze ad performance, and improve the ad experience. You can review Unity's full privacy policy at Unity Privacy Policy.

Personalized vs. Non-Personalized Ads:

  • Personalized Ads: Based on your interests, as inferred by your activity on other apps or websites.
  • Non-Personalized Ads: Targeted using only contextual information, such as your general location (e.g., city-level data) or app content.

You can manage your ad preferences or opt-out of personalized ads by visiting the Google Ads Settings and Unity's Privacy Policy.

Payment Information

If you make a purchase through the App Store, payment processing is handled solely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information or transaction details. For more information, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Your Privacy Choices

  • Manage your Google Ad preferences via the Google Ads Settings.
  • Manage your Unity Ads preferences via the Unity Privacy Policy.
  • Opt-out of data collection for personalized ads where applicable.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our app, third-party integrations, or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
