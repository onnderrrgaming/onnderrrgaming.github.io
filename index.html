About the Game

Welcome to Diamond Blocks DI Game, the ultimate block puzzle game! Strategically place blocks on the grid to create and clear rows or columns, earning points and progressing to higher levels. With stunning graphics and addictive gameplay, Diamond Blocks DI Game is a must-play for puzzle enthusiasts!

  • Challenging levels to test your skills
  • Compete with friends and players worldwide
